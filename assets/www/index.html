"Where's My Refund?" is a resource provided by RefundSchedule.com to more easily help mobile users find the date that they can expect their tax refund. This tool is constantly updated to keep it precise and accurate. This tool uses information from our IRS Refund Cycle Chart and we have included a link on the app main page. Refund information for the most current tax year you filed will generally be available within 24 hours after we acknowledge receipt of your e-filed return. If you filed a paper return, please allow 4 weeks before checking the status. The "Where's My Refund?" tool doesn't include information regarding State Tax Refunds. We do have a webpage that has this information available though. Details here: Where's My State Refund.
DISCLAIMER:
This app is in no way affiliated with the Internal Revenue Service. It is purely for informational purposes to help individuals better plan their taxes. These are only estimates, the I.R.S. has refused to give exact dates to new audit process. There are no guarantees with the I.R.S this year, but one thing is for sure: The earlier you file, the earlier you will receive a return. Check the status of your Tax Return on the I.R.S.’s Where’s My Refund tool.
